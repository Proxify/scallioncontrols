Vibe Dicing Platform
Don't describe what you want. Describe how you feel.

Vibe Dicing represents a paradigm shift in onion preparation. Instead of specifying precise cuts, dimensions, or techniques, simply describe the emotional energy you want your onions to embody, and our AI agent handles the rest.

How It Works:
1. You describe a vibe (e.g., "Sunday morning farmer's market energy")
2. OnionGPT-o3 spends 47 minutes interpreting your vibe through Chain-of-Shallot reasoning
3. The Vibe Dicing agent translates the reasoning into precise knife commands
4. Your onions are diced in a way that emotionally resonates with your original intent
5. Results may vary. That's the vibe.

Pre-Built Vibe Templates:
  • "Rustic" — Irregular cuts, warm imperfection, one piece suspiciously large
  • "Corporate" — Perfect 6mm cubes, uniform, soulless, ready for a board meeting
  • "Chaotic Neutral" — Agent enters a creative fugue state. Beautiful but unreplicable.
  • "First Date" — Delicate, trying too hard, slightly overthought
  • "3 AM Diner" — Aggressive, efficient, no questions asked
  • "The Beaumont" — RESTRICTED. Requires written authorization from legal. NDA mandatory. Fire extinguisher must be within arm's reach.

  • Signed liability waiver (standard) or enhanced liability waiver ("The Beaumont" template)

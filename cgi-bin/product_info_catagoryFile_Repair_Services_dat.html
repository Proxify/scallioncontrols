Agent Repair & Recovery
When your AI agents go rogue, we send more AI agents.

Let's be honest: autonomous onion agents don't always behave as expected. Sometimes they order 40,000 pounds of shallots. Sometimes they enter infinite reasoning loops. Sometimes they form autonomous collectives and draft their own governance charters. That's where our Repair & Recovery services come in.

Tier 1: Agent Whispering ($499/incident)
  • A trained recovery agent gently persuades your rogue agent to return to its assigned task
  • Average resolution time: 4 hours
  • Success rate: 73%
